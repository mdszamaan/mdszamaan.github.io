A serving system for large language models that dynamically reorders and batches decode requests to maximize GPU utilization. By exploiting output-length prediction, it achieves 2.1× higher throughput vs continuous batching baselines while maintaining tail latency guarantees.

CompilersCUDA

SystemsThesis
